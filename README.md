# 1. Who is this website for?

-Algerian people looking for all kinds of jobs, and others hiring.

## 1.1 Targeted individuals:

### What is the age range of your target audience?

Above 18 years old.

### Will your site appeal to more women or men? What is the mix?

All genders will have the same experience/interest in the website so gender is irrelevant here.

### Which country do your visitors live in?

Algeria.

### Do they live in urban or rural areas?

Residents of all areas can use the website.

### What is the average income of visitors?

People with any type of income can use the website as long as they can afford a small fee inscription fee.

### What level of education do they have?

For seekers, it depends on the job they're looking for , and the conditions (needed qualifications) set by the providers.
For providers, it varies.

### What is their occupation?

While seekers can be unemployed and looking for jobs, they can also be employed and looking for additional work.
And for providers, it varies from business men/ and company/organization owners, to anyone looking for a simple service such as housekeeping, babysitting...etc

### How many hours do they work per week?

It varies from user to user.

### How often do they use the web?

they are used to accessing the web for their needs. And can do basic searches, signups, etc..

### What kind of device do they use to access the web?

Phones and tablets, laptops and computers.

## 1.2 Targeted companies:

### What is the size of the company or relevant department?

Any hiring company of any size. It doesn't matter.

### What is the position of people in the company who visit your site?

Usually recruiters, but any position can do.

### Will visitors be using the site for themselves or for someone else?

Seekers use the site to find jobs for themselves
However, providers can also be company members who were assigned the task of finding employees, or family members/ friends that were asked to find them.
So generally, both cases are possible.

### How large is the budget they control?

It depends on each individual/company.

# 4. What information visitors need to achieve their goals?

### Will visitors be familiar with your subject area / brand or do you need to introduce yourself?

Even though they are familiar with the concept of offering/getting a job, they still need a brief introduction to the way our website works and what it provides.

### Will they be familiar with the product / service / information you are covering or do they need background information on it?

yes they're familiar.

### What are the most important features of what you are offering?

-   Facilitating the process of getting in touch with people that are hiring and others that are looking for jobs, as they can contact each other via our website through chats.
-   Showing people customized/filtered results of job-offers/applicants relevant to their picks and domain/category of interest and career paths, which saves them time.
-   Both seekers and providers can post job positions/Applications to show up on the other's feed.
-   Rating/feedback features that help the user make the right choices.
-   Alerts whenever the user's posts gets interacted with.
-   Finding several people's CVs with no effort , the process of reading them is rendered easy thanks to the waiting list feature (a "Check later" for CVs)
-   On the other hand, Having your CV's viewed by job owners is a trivial process of just posting an application.

### What is special about what you offer that differentiates you from other sites that offer something similar?

Our website is the first one of its kind in Algeria, plus it's made for all Adult Algerians and for all kinds of jobs and services. Therefore its inclusivity is unique.

### Once people have achieved the goal that sent them to your site, are there common questions people ask about this subject area?

no, once they connect with the other party. It's their personal matter and we are no longer involved.

# 5. How often will people visit the website?

They will continuously visit the website until they find the needed jobs (seekers), or find workers for the positions provided (providers).
